DAVC or Dayanand Anglo-Vedic College, Chandigarh was established in 1958. It is affiliated to Panjab University located in Chandigarh.DAV College Chandigarh offers 8 variants in the degree of Bachelors, 12 subjects for Masters and 3 for Ph.D. along with Diploma in Cosmetology and Beauty care, Advance Diploma in Medical Lab Technology, and P.G. Diploma in Computer Applications, Mass Communication, and Marketing Management.Admissions are completely based on merit and marks. The forms for admission are available online while the entrance tests are conducted both online and offline.

About us

DAV College is a co-educational college in Chandigarh, India. It was founded in 1958. Affiliated to the Panjab University, Chandigarh, the college offers various courses at undergraduate and postgraduate level. It is situated in Sector-10, Chandigarh near Hotel Mountview
